My Hobbies

Music

Music has been a lifelong excitement. Here's some highlights:

  • Rockband: As a teenager I wrote my own songs and played concerts with my rockbands.
  • Partyband: After high school I formed a Disney tribute band with some friends. We have entertained with nostalgic and catchy tunes at countless parties awakening the inner child of many.
  • Choir: At university I was trained conducting choirs and have since conducted many amateur choirs.
  • Musical: Hanging out with both music and theatre students I developed an interest in musical theatre and started writing and performing original musical shows together with fellow students. I wanna create more musicals and right now I'm attending musical shows and listening to a lot of musicals to learn from the best.

Personal Development

In 2017 I went through some challenging experiences that made me reflect on my way of being in this world and started a new interest in personal development.

  • Personality Types: While struggling writing my master thesis I was introduced to Myers Briggs. It blew my mind and a lot of struggles made sense.
  • Coaching: I completed a coaching course to become a better high school teacher. I likes it so much that I quit my job to do it full time.
  • Speaking: I went through a public speaking training to share my story. I wanna inspire people to listen to the heart and do what it takes to create a life of joy.
  • Ideas: I listen to a lot of audiobooks and podcasts to feed myself new ideas on broadening my perspective.
  • Systems: I have a very conceptual mind and enjoy systems that describes aspects of human life - especially typologies like Myers Briggs, the Enneagram, Love Languages and Attachment Styles. Ego work and relational practises also have my interest.

Art Festivals

I love magical experiences with kind people in extravagent invironments. Since 2018 I have been part of the Scandinavian Burning Man community co-creating big and small events with spectacular content.

  • Costume Tent: A tent full of outfits and glitter for everyone to dress up as fancy and sparkly they like.
  • Kitchen: Taking lead on food plans and shopping.
  • Performance: Performing at cabarets and no-talent shows with my music comedy group The Miaws.
  • Candy Clouds: A soft and sweet space full of pillows and candy.
